Introduction to Crocheting

I know, I know. Crocheting is for grandmas. BUT, I'm here to show that it doesn't have to be an old person thing. Crocheting is very relaxing and you can make so many amazing things by crocheting. It's not just for the Baby Boomers. It's for Millennials and Gen Zers too.

To get started, here are some well-known stitches to get someone right on their way to being a proficient crocheter.

Common Stitches

  • Chain
    •

    This is the base to all crocheting. It is the beginning of every project and has the abbreviation of ch. Click here to learn more.

  • Single Crochet
    •

    This is the most basic stitch and is very commonly used. The abbreviation of this stich is sc. Click here to learn more.

  • Double Crochet
    •

    This is another common stitch in crocheting. It is similar to the single crochet, it's just double the size for a longer stitch. The abbreviation for this stitch is dc. Click here to learn more.
