The Silkie Chicken

About the Silkie

The Silkie Chicken is a fun, family favorite. This docile chicken is perfect for a backyard coop.

Fun Facts

Silkies are very unique chickens. Here are some interesting things about a Silkie.

  • They lay fewer eggs than other breeds ranging from 90-120 a year.
  • They make great setters and are often used to hatch other breeds eggs.
  • They are calm and trusting.
  • Yes, they actually look like cotton balls.
